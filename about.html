ABOUT US

Packed to the rafters with character, and more than a hint of mischief, The Old Frizzle on The Broadway opposite Wimbledon Theatre, begs to be discovered. Paying homage to life’s simple pleasures, flavoursome yet classic classic pub food and a wide selection of beers, wines and cocktails are served up in eccentrically cosy surroundings, comprising of overflowing bookshelves, vintage lamps, Chesterfield sofas, secretive snugs and, yes, that’s a squash court ceiling.

Perfect sports viewing, live music, delicious roasts, recovery brunches and the infamous Frizzle Quizzle every Sunday night makes it the perfect convivial spot in which to hunker down at weekends enjoying the company of good friends in a charming setting. We are very partial to a party, and would love to host yours. Get in touch, tell us what you need, and let us do the legwork. With a myriad of areas in all sizes available to book, The Old Frizzle is bound to be the perfect spot for your next shindig. To book, simply call 020 3274 3056, use our Party Starter Form, or stop by for a good old-fashioned chat.
